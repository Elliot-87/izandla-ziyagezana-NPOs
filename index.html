Our Mission

Izandla Ziyagezana NPO was established in 2023 with the vision of addressing food insecurity and clothing needs within the community. Recognizing the growing challenges faced by poor families, the homeless and ordinary individuals, a group of passionate individuals came together to create a sustainable and community-driven solution.

Daily Meal Programs

The main objectives of the Izandla Ziyagezana NPO are to empower communities and help alleviate poverty in poverty stricken communities.

Clothing Distribution

Collecting and distributing donated clothes to the less fortunate. Sustainable Livelihood Projects – Training members in agriculture, food processing, and small-scale entrepreneurship.

Community Support

Helping communities through different projects, such as, feeding scheme (focusing mostly on children, pensioners and people with chronic illnesses), agricultural training, clothing give aways.

Daily Meal Programs

Our community kitchens serve over 500 nutritious meals daily to children, elderly, and chronically ill individuals.

  • 3 community kitchens operating daily
  • 87% of ingredients sourced locally
  • Nutritionist-designed menus

Clothing Distribution

We've distributed over 2,400 clothing items to those in need while promoting sustainable fashion practices.

  • Monthly distribution events
  • Winter coat drives
  • School uniform assistance

Community Support

Our holistic approach includes agricultural training, skills development, and mental health support.

  • 12 sustainable farming graduates last quarter
  • Weekly support groups
  • Job placement assistance
